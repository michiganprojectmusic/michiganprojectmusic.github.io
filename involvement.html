Get Involved!

Membership

We are accepting new members of all majors and backgrounds. Whether you are looking to build an instrument that has never been made before, join your peers and play in a band, or both, Project Music is excited to have you. We have build and design sessions once to twice a week and bands tend to practice once to twice a week. No experience is necessary to join our build team and we're always looking for people with new ideas and outlooks.

If you have any questions, email our executive board at ProjectMusicExecs16-17@umich.edu or join our club from MaizePages, found here.

Sponsorship

Project Music is proudly funded by University of Michigan's Department of Physics and Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering. We are always available for additional support. If you would like to sponsor Project Music, please email our executive board at ProjectMusicExecs16-17@umich.edu.
